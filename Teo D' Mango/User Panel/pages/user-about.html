About Teo D' Mango

Crafting unforgettable mango experiences with passion, quality, and a touch of sweetness

Who We Are

Teo D' Mango is more than just a mango shake business - we're a passionate team dedicated to bringing the authentic taste of premium Philippine mangoes to every community. Founded with a simple vision, we've grown from a single stall to a beloved local brand known for quality and innovation.

Our commitment goes beyond serving delicious drinks. We strive to create moments of joy and refreshment, one mango shake at a time, while supporting local mango farmers and sustainable practices.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make premium mango creations accessible to everyone by using only the highest quality ingredients, maintaining affordable prices, and providing exceptional customer experiences in every interaction.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and beloved mango specialty brand in the Philippines, known for innovation, quality, and our commitment to community and sustainability.

Our Values

Quality

We use only the freshest, highest-grade mangoes and ingredients in all our products.

Passion

Our love for mangoes drives us to create the most delicious and innovative recipes.

Community

We believe in building strong relationships with our customers and supporting local communities.

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices and supporting local mango farmers.
