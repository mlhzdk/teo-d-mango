Our Story

The Beginning of Something Sweet

Teo D' Mango began in 2022 with a simple vision: to bring the authentic taste of fresh Philippine mangoes to everyone. What started as a small kiosk in Lemery has grown into a beloved brand known for quality and authenticity.

Our founder, Teodoro Mendoza III, grew up surrounded by mango orchards in Lemery, Philippines. He learned early on that the secret to a perfect mango shake wasn't just in the recipe, but in the quality of the mangoes themselves.

Growing Together

As word spread about our delicious, authentic mango shakes, we expanded to multiple locations while maintaining our commitment to quality. Each new store brought us closer to communities who shared our love for fresh, natural flavors.

Today, we continue to source our mangoes directly from Philippine farmers, ensuring every shake carries the authentic taste that started our journey.

Our Promise to You

We believe that everyone deserves to experience the joy of premium mango shakes made with real, fresh ingredients. No artificial flavors, no shortcuts - just the genuine taste of ripe mangoes in every sip.
