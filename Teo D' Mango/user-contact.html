Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery options?

We offer delivery through popular food delivery platforms in your area. You can also call us directly for pickup orders.

Do you offer catering for events?

Yes! We provide catering services for events of all sizes. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your needs.

Are your mango shakes available year-round?

Yes, we source premium Philippine mangoes year-round to ensure our signature mango shakes are always available.

Do you sell anything besides mangoes?

Yes! We serve fresh avocado, coconuts and strawberries.

About Teo D' Mango

We believe that premium mango shakes should be accessible to everyone, not just a luxury for a few. Our mission is to bring high-quality, naturally sweet mango creations to communities everywhere.
